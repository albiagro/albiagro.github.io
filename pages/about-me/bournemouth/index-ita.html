Why do I love English?

As I wrote here, I had an incredible experience in Bournemouth (UK) in July 2016, at the end of High School.

The project was called "Newly graduated talents" (by CRT Foundation, Turin) and I was been chosen from all the students at the school to spend 3 months in the UK, in order to improve my English skills.

I had English lessons for 2 weeks, while the rest of the time I worked as a receptionist at Kings School (where my lessons took place).

It has been really helpful to improve my English and interpersonal skills!
